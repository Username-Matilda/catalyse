What is a tension? 📖

Constitution Article 1.1: A tension is your perception of a gap between current reality and a potential you sense. It's not a problem to solve—it's an opportunity to explore.

Good examples:

  • "The Fermentation Lead lacks authority over refrigerator space allocation" (governance)
  • "Our worm bin pH is 9.2 and they're actively protesting" (tactical)
  • "Unclear who approves new microorganism acquisitions after the kombucha incident" (governance)
  • "The tool shed is chaos since the Great Hoe Incident of Q3" (tactical)

Not-as-good examples:

  • "We need better processes" (too vague—what specific gap?)
  • "Sarah should manage the greenhouse" (solution, not tension)
