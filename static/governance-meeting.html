📖 Governance Meeting Process (Article 3)

Purpose: Process tensions into structural changes to improve organizational capacity.

The process (one tension at a time):

  • 1. Present Proposal - Proposer describes structural change
  • 2. Clarifying Questions - Information seeking only, no reactions
  • 3. Reaction Round - Each person shares perspective, no discussion
  • 4. Amend & Clarify - Proposer may modify based on reactions
  • 5. Objection Round - Test for harm: would this cause damage or move backwards?
  • 6. Integration - If objections exist, work together to integrate
Valid Objection Test:
  • ❓ Would this proposal cause harm?
  • ❓ Would it move us backwards?
  • ❓ Does it create a new tension worse than the original?
NOT valid objections:
  • ✗ "I have a better idea" (offer after adoption)
  • ✗ "I don't like it" (reaction, not objection)
  • ✗ "We tried this before" (past ≠ future)
