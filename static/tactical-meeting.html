📖 Tactical Meeting Purpose (Article 4)

Purpose: Synchronize team work, not change structure. Focus on operational coordination and next actions.

The flow:

  • Check-in: Brief personal sharing (1-2 sentences, no discussion)
  • Checklist: Review recurring operational items
  • Metrics: Share key numbers that inform decisions
  • Projects: Quick updates on multi-step initiatives
  • Tensions: Process operational gaps → identify next actions
  • Closing: Brief reflection or appreciation

Not governance: Don't change roles/accountabilities here—capture those tensions for governance meeting.
